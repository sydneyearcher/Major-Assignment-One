Dark Wave Movement

Welcome to the Dark Wave Movement archive. On this site we'll be exploring everything about the Dark Wave Movement including history, popular artists then and now, and the rich culture surrounding the genre. The term "dark wave" began to suffarce in the early 1980s and emerged during the post punk movemen. Its parent genre is gothic rock. Dark wave adopted a more broad definition, a blend of synthesizer based music and gothic rock which creates a perfect mix of eerie and psychedelic.

Since the 2010's, Dark Wave has had a huge revival, some more recent bands that specilize in Dark Wave or have Dark Wave songs are Puzzle, Crystal Castles, Mareux, Machine Girl, TV Girl, Have a Nice Life, The Smiths, Eyedress, Molchat Doma, Poison Tree and My Bloody Valentine.

Dark Wave Facts

-The term "Dark Wave" originated in Germany.

-Popular artists of the genre (new and old) may include some people you never expected like: She Wants Revenge,The Cocteau Twins, The Cure, Joy Wave, The Birthday Part, The Chameleons, Siouxie and The Banshees, Depeche Mode and many more!

-Dark Wave is one of 5 subgenres of New Wave music. The rest of these subgenres include, Cold Wave, Synth Wave, Etheral Wave, Neoclassical Dark Wave.

-Dark Wave is also known as Doom Wave.

-Dark Waves'subgenre is Etheral Wave music.
